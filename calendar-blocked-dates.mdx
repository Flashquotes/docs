## What blocked dates do

Blocked dates to prevent new inquiries and automatically expire open quotes on those dates.

**What happens when you block a date:**
- Customers can't select it on lead intake forms
- Open quotes on that date expire immediately
- Date appears unavailable in your calendar

## How to block dates

### Single date
1. Go to Calendar → Click **Block out dates**
2. Click a date
3. Add reason (optional)
4. Click **Block Date**

### Multiple dates
1. Go to Calendar → Click **Block out dates**
2. Click multiple dates
3. Add reason (optional)
4. Click **Block Dates**

## Quote expiration warning

**Before blocking:** System shows warning if dates have open quotes
**After blocking:** Quotes expire immediately 

## Managing blocked dates

**View:** Blocked dates appear grayed out in calendar
**Remove:** Click blocked date → **Unblock Date** → Confirm

<Warning>
Unblocking doesn't automatically restore expired quotes for that date.
</Warning>

## FAQ

**Can I block past dates?** No, only current and future dates.

**Do booked events get affected?** No, only open quotes expire.

**Do I need to add a reason?** No, it's optional.
