The command palette is the fastest way to move around Flashquotes when you're running an event. Open it from any authenticated page, type a few characters, and hit **Enter**.

## Open the palette

- **Mac:** <kbd>⌘</kbd> <kbd>K</kbd>
- **Windows / Linux:** <kbd>Ctrl</kbd> <kbd>K</kbd>
- Or click **Search** at the top of the sidebar

<Tip>
  The shortcut works from anywhere in the app — including inside a quote editor or booking detail — so you never have to reach for the nav.
</Tip>

## What you can find

### Pages

Type any page name to jump straight to it, including nested Settings pages.

- `tax` surfaces both the **Sales Tax** report and **Settings → Tax Rates**
- `quickbooks` jumps you to **Settings → Integrations → QuickBooks**
- `payouts`, `calendar`, `staff` — anything in the sidebar is one keystroke away

### Records

Search across your data without leaving the page you're on:

- **Leads** — by name, email, or phone digits (e.g. `5551234`)
- **Quotes** — by quote number or lead name
- **Invoices** — by invoice number, amount, or lead name
- **Bookings** — by event date, lead name, or booking number

### Quick actions

Run common actions from anywhere:

- **New lead**
- **New quote**
- **Connect Gmail**

Pages you're currently on can register their own actions too — for example, opening a quote adds **Send quote** and related shortcuts to the palette.

## Keyboard navigation

| Key | Action |
| --- | --- |
| <kbd>↑</kbd> / <kbd>↓</kbd> | Move between results |
| <kbd>↵</kbd> | Open the highlighted result |
| <kbd>Esc</kbd> | Close the palette |

<Note>
  Recently opened leads, quotes, and pages float to the top of the results, so the records you touch most often are always one keystroke away.
</Note>
