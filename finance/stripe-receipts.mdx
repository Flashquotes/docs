# View Stripe payment receipts

Access official Stripe receipts for any payment to provide customers with detailed transaction records.

## Find receipt links

Receipt links appear next to payment details in these locations:

1. **Billing tab** - In the payments table
2. **Invoice tab** - Next to each payment entry

Look for the "Receipt" link beside the "Details" button.

## Access receipts

Steps:
1. Go to billing or invoice tab
2. Find the payment in the table
3. Click "Receipt" next to "Details"
4. Receipt opens in new tab

What happens next: The official Stripe receipt loads with complete transaction details, ready for printing or saving.

## What's included

Stripe receipts show:
- Payment amount and date
- Payment method details
- Transaction ID
- Business information
- Customer billing details

## Email receipts

Stripe automatically sends a receipt to the customer's email address when a payment is processed. If a customer says they didn't receive their receipt, you can use the Receipt link to access and resend it directly from the Stripe receipt page.
