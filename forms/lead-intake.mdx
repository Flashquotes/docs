## Overview

The Lead Intake Form is optimized for high conversion rates, collecting only essential information needed to generate accurate quotes. This form automatically generates when you create your account and can be embedded on your website.

## Form Structure

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="page" title="Page-by-Page Breakdown">
    The lead intake form is optimized with minimal, essential questions:

    1. **Service Details**
       - Service length
       - Guest count
       - Dynamic staff calculation

    2. **Location**
       - Event address
       - Venue details

    3. **Timing**
       - Event date
       - Service start time
       - Number of event days

    4. **Event Type**
       - Category selection
       - Pricing impact factors

    5. **Customization**
       - Add-ons selection
       - Special requirements

    6. **Additional Information**
       - Event details
       - Referral source

    7. **Contact Details**
       - First name
       - Last name
       - Email
       - Phone number
       - SMS opt-in consent

  </Accordion>

<Accordion icon="message-circle" title="SMS Opt-in Configuration">
  Enable SMS opt-in collection on your lead intake form: 1. Navigate to your
  lead intake form editor 2. Go to the Contact Details page settings 3. Expand
  the "SMS Opt-in" section 4. Toggle "Enable SMS opt-in" to activate the feature
  When enabled: - A checkbox appears asking "Can we text you?" - Leads can
  opt-in to receive SMS messages about their quote and event - Opt-in status is
  tracked in lead management - SMS badge appears on lead cards for opted-in
  contacts
  <Note>
    SMS opt-in is only available on forms that include a phone number field.
  </Note>
  <Tip>
    Customize the question title and acknowledgement text to match your brand
    voice and compliance requirements.
  </Tip>
</Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="bolt" title="Instant Quoting">
    Enable automatic quote generation and presentation:
    
    1. Click the "Instant Pricing" button in form settings
    2. Toggle "Quoting Mode" to "Instant Quoting"
    3. Configure fallback service and pricing rules
    
    **How pricing works:**
    - **With service selection questions**: Uses the service the customer selects
    - **Without service selection questions**: Uses the fallback service (fixed pricing)
    - **No service selected**: Falls back to the configured default service
    
    When enabled:
    - Quotes are generated instantly upon form submission
    - Clients are redirected to the quote presentation page
    - "Book Now" option is immediately available
    
    <Note>
      Instant Quoting is a [Pro plan](https://app.flashquotes.com/settings/plans) feature that enables clients to inquire, receive quotes, and book in a single session.
    </Note>

    <Tip>
      Combine Instant Quoting with [Quote View V2](/leads/quote-view-v2) for the ultimate conversion-optimized quote experience.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Website Integration

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="code" title="Form Embedding">
    To embed the lead intake form: 1. Click the `Embed Form` button 2. Select
    your website builder 3. Copy the provided code 4. Paste into your website's
    contact form location
    <Info>
      Flashquotes forms are compatible with virtually all website platforms. See
      our [Platform Compatibility](/forms/compatibility) guide for details on
      embedding in specific platforms.
    </Info>
    <Warning>
      Wix websites require specific embedding code. Select 'Wix' from the
      dropdown for correct code.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
