## Overview

The Staffing Calculator automatically calculates and displays the number of staff members needed based on guest count and service duration. This helps set customer expectations and justify pricing.

<Info>
For more accurate staffing, configure the [Staff & Resource Rules](/services/staff-resource-rules) at the service level instead. Service-level rules support four calculator modes and take priority over these form-level settings when configured.
</Info>

## Configuration Options

### Service Length
- **Minimum Hours**: Set the shortest service duration you offer
- **Maximum Hours**: Set the longest service duration available
- Supports half-hour increments for flexibility

### Guest Count Controls
- **Increment Value**: Set how much the +/- buttons change the count (1-100)
- Smaller increments (5-10) work well for intimate gatherings
- Larger increments (25-50) are better for corporate events

### Staffing Ratios
- **Service Capacity**: Define how many drinks/services one staff member can handle per hour
- **Maximum Staff**: Optionally cap the number of staff shown (leave empty for unlimited)

## Staff Visual Counter

The visual counter shows interactive staff images on your form that adjust as customers change their guest count and service length.

### Where to find it

1. Go to **Forms** and open your lead intake form in the editor.
2. Under **Content**, select the **Staffing Calculator** question.
3. In the settings panel, expand the **Staff visual counter** section.

### Turn the visual counter on or off

Use the **Enable visual counter** toggle:

- **On** — the form displays an interactive counter with staff images that adjusts to guest count. Customization fields appear below the toggle.
- **Off** — the form shows a text-only question instead. Customize the question title and subtitle; staffing calculations still happen behind the scenes.

<Note>
There is no separate "mode" switch or "Display Options" menu — the **Enable visual counter** toggle inside the Staffing Calculator question's settings is the only control.
</Note>

### Customize staff titles and photos

With the visual counter enabled, you can personalize:

- **Staff title (singular / plural)** — e.g. "barista" / "baristas"
- **Staff description** — e.g. "Serving lattes, cappuccinos, and more"
- **Staff Images** — click any of the five image slots to upload or select a photo of your team. Images are optional; hover a photo and click the X to remove it.

<Tip>
Want to replace the default staff icon with a photo of your own team? Enable the visual counter, then click an image slot under **Staff Images** to upload your photo.
</Tip>
