## Overview

Leads in Flashquotes represent potential customers who have expressed interest in your services. A lead can be created either automatically through your website's lead intake form or manually within the application. Each lead contains essential contact information and can be associated with quotes, making it easy to track your sales pipeline.

## Creating Leads

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="user-plus" title="Manual Lead Creation">
    To manually create a lead:
    
    1. Navigate to the [Leads](https://app.flashquotes.com/leads) page
    2. Click the `+ New Lead` button in the top right
    3. Fill in the required fields:
       - First Name
       - Last Name
       - Email
    4. Optional fields include:
       - Phone Number
       - City
       - Company Name

  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="globe" title="Automatic Lead Creation">
    Leads are automatically created when potential customers submit your lead intake form embedded on your website. These leads will include:
    
    - Contact information
    - Event details
    - A pre-built quote awaiting pricing

    <Tip>
      Learn how to embed the lead intake form in the [Forms section](/quickstart#forms)
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Managing Leads

### Lead Status Filtering

The Leads dashboard allows you to filter leads by their current status:

- **Total Leads**: View all leads within a selected date range
- **Open Inquiries**: Leads who haven't received a quote yet
- **Quoted Leads**: Leads who have received a quote but haven't booked
- **Booked Leads**: Leads who have received a quote and completed a booking

### Tracking Performance

<Info>
  The Leads dashboard includes valuable metrics like booking percentage, helping you track what percentage of quoted leads convert to bookings.
</Info>

### Lead Organization

Leads are organized chronologically and can be filtered by date range. Each lead entry displays:

- Contact information
- Quote status (if applicable)
- Booking status
- Date created
- Last activity

## Best Practices

1. Respond to new leads promptly to maintain engagement
2. Use the status filters to prioritize follow-ups
3. Monitor booking percentages to optimize your quote strategy
4. Keep lead information up-to-date for accurate reporting
