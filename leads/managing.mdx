## Overview

The Leads section in Flashquotes helps you track and manage potential clients from initial inquiry through quote acceptance. Each lead contains essential event details and client information needed for quote generation.

## Lead Management

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="inbox" title="Lead Dashboard">
    Monitor leads through:
    - New lead notifications
    - Status tracking
    - Follow-up reminders
    - Conversion metrics
    
    <Tip>
      Use status indicators to quickly identify leads requiring attention.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="user" title="Lead Information">
    Access key details:
    - Contact information
    - Event requirements
    - Date preferences
    - Service requests
    - Guest count
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Lead Processing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="arrows-split-up-and-left" title="Lead Sources">
    Track leads from:
    - Website form submissions
    - Manual entry
    - Phone inquiries
    - Partner referrals
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="timeline" title="Lead Journey">
    Follow leads through:
    - Initial inquiry
    - Quote generation
    - Client communication
    - Quote acceptance
    
    <Note>
      Each stage is tracked with timestamps for performance monitoring.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Best Practices

1. Respond to leads promptly
2. Keep lead information current
3. Track communication history
4. Monitor conversion rates
5. Follow up consistently
