## Getting Started

### Step 1: Enable Quote View V2

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Quote Settings">
    Go to **Settings ‚Üí Quote Settings** in your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the V2 Banner">
    Look for the "Try Quote Page V2" banner at the top of the page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Toggle Active">
    Toggle the switch to **Active**. Your quotes will now use the new V2
    template.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame>
  <img
    src="/images/leads/quote-v2/v2-opt-in-banner.webp"
    alt="Quote View V2 opt-in banner in Quote Settings"
  />
</Frame>

<Note>
  Quote View V2 is currently in beta. You can switch back to the classic view at
  any time.
</Note>

## Configuring Quote View V2 Features

### üì∏ Gallery Images

The gallery is one of the most impactful features for building trust and showcasing your services.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="/images/leads/quote-v2/gallery-screenshot.webp"
    alt="Gallery Images configuration interface"
  />
</Frame>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="upload" title="Setting Up Your Gallery">
    1. In the template editor, find the **Gallery Images** section
    2. Click on any of the 8 image slots
    3. Select an image from your media library or upload a new one
    4. The first image (top-left) will be marked as "Main" and displayed prominently
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="lightbulb" title="Best Practices">
    - Use high-quality images at 1200√ó900px (4:3 ratio) for best results
    - Include a mix of:
      - Final service results
      - Your team in action
      - Equipment and setup shots
      - Happy customers (with permission)
    - Update images seasonally to keep content fresh
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### ‚úÖ Service Details

Help customers understand exactly what's included in their quote with dynamic service details.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="/images/leads/quote-v2/service-details-screenshot.webp"
    alt="Service Details configuration interface"
  />
</Frame>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="list" title="Configuring Service Details">
    1. Open the **Service Details** section
    2. Click "Add Service Detail" to create a new item
    3. Use plain text or markdown formatting for rich content
    4. Include dynamic values with shortcodes
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="code" title="Available Shortcodes">
    - `{{quote.guest_count}}` - Number of guests
    - `{{quote.staff_count}}` - Number of staff
    - `{{quote.service_duration_hours}}` - Service duration
    - `{{quote.event_address}}` - Event location
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="file-text" title="Example Service Details">
    - "Professional service for `{{quote.guest_count}}` guests"
    - "`{{quote.staff_count}}` experienced staff members"
    - "All equipment and supplies included"
    - "`[View our safety protocols](https://yoursite.com/safety)`"
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### ‚≠ê Reviews & Testimonials

Social proof is crucial for building trust with potential customers.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="/images/leads/quote-v2/social-proof-settings.webp"
    alt="Reviews and Testimonials configuration interface"

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="star" title="Setting Up Reviews">
    1. In the **Reviews & Testimonials** section, toggle "Reviews" on

    3. Enter your total review count (e.g., 1,348)
  </Accordion>

{" "}

<Accordion icon="quote" title="Adding Testimonials">
  1. Click "Add testimonial" 2. Enter the customer quote and name 3. Drag to
  reorder testimonials 4. Add 3-5 compelling testimonials for best results
</Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="trophy" title="Pro Tips">
    - Use specific, detailed testimonials that mention results
    - Include the customer's first name and last initial
    - Rotate testimonials periodically to keep them current
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### üí∞ Add-ons Configuration

Add-ons are your opportunity to increase average order value while providing valuable options to customers.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="/images/leads/quote-v2/addons-screenshot.webp"
    alt="Add-ons configuration interface"
  />

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="settings" title="Managing Add-ons">
    1. In the **Add-ons** section, you'll see all your created add-ons
    2. Toggle add-ons on/off to control visibility
    3. Drag to reorder - put your most popular add-ons first
    4. Click the edit icon to update add-on details
  </Accordion>

{" "}

<Accordion icon="chart-line" title="Creating Effective Add-ons">
  - Use clear, benefit-focused names - Include appetizing photos - Price
  strategically (not too high to deter, not too low to devalue) - Limit to 4-8
  options to avoid overwhelming customers
</Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="calculator" title="Dynamic Pricing Options">
    When creating or editing add-ons, you can set pricing that automatically adjusts based on:
    - Number of guests
    - Service duration
    - Number of days
    - Number of resources/staff
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="arrow-left" title="Can I switch back to the classic quote view?">
    Yes, simply toggle off Quote View V2 in your Quote Settings at any time.
  </Accordion>

{" "}

<Accordion icon="file" title="Will my existing quotes be affected?">
  No, only new quotes sent after enabling V2 will use the new template.
</Accordion>

{" "}

<Accordion icon="eye" title="Can I preview my changes before they go live?">
  Yes, the template editor shows a real-time preview of your quote page as you
  make changes.
</Accordion>

{" "}

<Accordion icon="plus" title="Do I need to recreate my add-ons for V2?">
  No, your existing add-ons work seamlessly with V2 - just choose which ones to
  display.
</Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="mobile" title="Is Quote View V2 mobile-friendly?">
    Absolutely! V2 is fully responsive and optimized for mobile booking.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
