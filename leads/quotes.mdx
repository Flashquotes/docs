## Overview

Quotes in Flashquotes allow you to provide detailed pricing and service information to your leads. Whether generated automatically from lead intake forms or created manually, quotes can be customized with pricing, services, and branded elements.

## Quote Creation and Management

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="file-plus" title="Creating a Quote">
    To create a new quote:

    1. Navigate to the [Leads](https://app.flashquotes.com/leads) page
    2. Click `+ New Quote` next to the relevant lead
    3. Fill in the required event details
    4. Click `Create`

    <Note>
      When leads come through your intake form, a pre-populated quote will be automatically created, requiring only pricing to be added.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="pen-to-square" title="Customizing Quotes">
    The quote editor allows you to customize:

    - Event service pricing
    - Guest count
    - Service start and end times
    - Tax rates
    - Special notes
    - Hero message and image
    - Additional line items and fees
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Quote Features

### Location and Travel Planning

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="map" title="Map Integration">
    Each quote includes an integrated map view showing:
    
    - Service location
    - Venue address
    - Drive time
    - Mileage calculation

    This helps ensure accurate travel cost estimation and proper profit margins.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Quote Customization

#### Visual Elements
- Customize hero images for each quote
- Set default hero images at the company level
- Modify hero text and messaging
- Brand your quotes with your company's style

#### Pricing and Terms
- Add line items for services
- Include travel costs
- Apply tax rates
- Add special notes and terms

## Best Practices

1. Review all pre-populated information for accuracy
2. Double-check travel costs and mileage
3. Keep pricing and terms clear and consistent
4. Use appropriate hero images and messaging
5. Verify all details before sending
