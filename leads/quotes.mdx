## Overview

Quotes in Flashquotes allow you to provide detailed pricing and service information to your leads. Whether generated automatically from lead intake forms or created manually, quotes can be customized with pricing, services, and branded elements.

## Quote Creation and Management

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="file-plus" title="Creating a Manual Quote">
    To create a new quote:

    1. Navigate to the [Leads](https://app.flashquotes.com/leads) page
    2. Click on the lead you want to create a quote for
    3. Click `+ New Quote`
    4. Fill in the required event details
    5. Click `Create`

  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="pen-to-square" title="Customizing Quotes">
    The quote editor allows you to customize the details and presentation of your quote. Customization options include:

    ### Key event details
    - Event address
    - Event date
    - Service start and end times
    - Guest count
    - Service location
    - Number of staff and core resources
    - Special notes

    <Tip>Most of this information is pre-populated from the lead intake form, but you can customize it to fit your needs.</Tip>

    #### Pricing and line items

    - Modify or add line items for services
    - Add line items for add-ons
    - Apply tax rates
    - Apply discounts
    - Add service descriptions

    #### Visual elements

    - Customize hero images for each quote
    - Modify hero text and messaging presented on the quote
    - Present the quote in a branded color scheme by [setting your brand colors](/settings/branding)

    <Info>
      You can also set a default hero image and messaging in your [quote settings](/settings/quotes).
    </Info>

  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Note>
  When leads come through your [lead intake form](/forms/lead-intake), a
  pre-populated quote will be automatically created, requiring only pricing to
  be added.
</Note>

## Quote Features

### Location and Travel Planning

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="map" title="Map Integration">
    Each quote includes an integrated map view showing:
    
    - Service location
    - Venue address
    - Drive time
    - Mileage calculation

    This helps ensure accurate travel cost estimation and proper profit margins.

  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Best Practices

1. Review all pre-populated information for accuracy

2. Keep pricing and terms clear and consistent
3. Use appropriate hero images and messaging
4. Add additional travel costs and mileage as neccesary using the [map integration](/leads/quotes#location-and-travel-planning)
5. Verify all details before sending
