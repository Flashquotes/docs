## Overview

The Settings section is crucial for initial setup and ongoing management of your Flashquotes account. Along with Forms configuration, it's one of the most important areas to focus on when getting started.

## General Settings

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="building" title="Company Information">
    Basic company details include:
    - Company name
    - Company ID
    - Invoice display name
    - Invoice address
    - Contact email
    - Contact phone
    
    <Note>
      Company and location IDs are used for building automations with platforms like Zapier.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="map-pin" title="Location Management">
    Configure your service locations:
    - Set primary headquarters
    - Define resource storage locations
    - Enable accurate timeline calculations
    - Manage multiple service areas
    
    <Tip>
      Set your location to where your resources are stored to ensure accurate drive time calculations.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Timeline Defaults

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="clock" title="Time Parameters">
    Configure default durations for: 1. **Loading Time** - Duration for packing
    and loading at headquarters 2. **Setup Time** - Duration for event location
    setup 3. **Cleanup Time** - Duration for post-service packup 4. **Unloading
    Time** - Duration for headquarters unloading and restocking
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Task Templates

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="list-check" title="Task Configuration">
    Manage two types of task templates: 1. **Admin Tasks** - Internal management
    tasks - Operational checklists 2. **Event Brief Tasks** - Section assignment
    - Task ordering - Markdown formatting support
    <Tip>
      Use Markdown for task formatting: `[Link Text](URL)` for hyperlinks
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

3. Configure timeline defaults accurately
4. Review and customize task templates
