## Overview

Add-on settings allow you to create and manage additional services that can be included in quotes and bookings. Each add-on can be configured with specific pricing models and automation IDs for integration purposes.

## Add-on Configuration

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="plus" title="Creating Add-ons">
    Set up new add-ons with:
    - Unique identifier
    - Display name
    - Description
    - Pricing structure
    - Availability rules
    
    <Note>
      Unique IDs are essential for automation workflows and integrations.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="money-bill" title="Pricing Options">
    Configure pricing models:
    - Per unit pricing
    - Flat rate pricing
    - Hourly rates
    - Resource-based pricing

      Choose pricing models that align with your operational costs and market positioning.

</AccordionGroup>
