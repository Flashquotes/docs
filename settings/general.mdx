## Overview

General settings contain your core company information and location configuration. This information is used throughout Flashquotes for invoicing, communications, and timeline calculations.

## Company Information

<AccordionGroup>
	<Accordion icon="building" title="Basic Details">
		Configure essential company information: - Company name - Company ID -
		Invoice display name - Invoice address - Contact email - Contact phone
		<Note>
			Company and location IDs are used for building automations with platforms
			like Zapier.
		</Note>
	</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Location Management

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="map-pin" title="Location Address">
    Set your headquarters location address to ensure accurate drive time calculations and service area validation.
    
    <Tip>
      Your primary location should be where your resources are stored to ensure accurate drive time calculations.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="clock" title="Location Timezone">
    Set your timezone to ensure accurate timeline and event time presentation.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="envelope" title="Default Email">
    Set your default email for each location to ensure the right email is used when sending a quote.

    <Tip>
      Need to add another account? Click *+ Add new email* to easily link a new Google account.
    </Tip>

  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="building-columns" title="Default Tax Rate">
    Set your default tax rate for each location to ensure the right tax rate is auto-applied to each quote.
  </Accordion>
  
</AccordionGroup>

## Best Practices

1. Keep company information current
2. Verify location accuracy
3. Use a default tax rate for each location
4. Use a default email for each location
