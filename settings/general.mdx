## Overview

General settings contain your core company information and location configuration. This information is used throughout Flashquotes for invoicing, communications, and timeline calculations.

## Company Information

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="building" title="Basic Details">
    Configure essential company information:
    - Company name
    - Company ID
    - Invoice display name
    - Invoice address
    - Contact email
    - Contact phone
    
    <Note>
      Company and location IDs are used for building automations with platforms like Zapier.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Location Management

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="map-pin" title="Primary Location">
    Set your headquarters location:
    - Physical address
    - Resource storage location
    - Timeline calculation origin
    - Service area center
    
    <Tip>
      Your primary location should be where your resources are stored to ensure accurate drive time calculations.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="map" title="Service Areas">
    Define your operational boundaries:
    - Service radius
    - Multiple locations
    - Coverage areas
    - Travel zones
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Best Practices

1. Keep company information current
2. Verify location accuracy
3. Update service areas as needed
4. Maintain correct contact details
5. Review automation IDs periodically
