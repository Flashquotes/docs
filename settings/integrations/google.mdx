## Overview

The Google integration allows you to send quotes and communications directly from your Gmail account. This creates a more personal connection with your leads and helps maintain your email communication history in one place.

## Setup Process

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="envelope" title="Connecting Gmail">
    To connect your Gmail account:
    1. Navigate to Settings > Integrations
    2. Find the Google section
    3. Click "+ Add account"
    4. Sign in to your Google account
    5. Grant necessary permissions
    
    <Note>
      You can connect multiple Gmail accounts if you need to send from different addresses.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="shield-check" title="Permissions">
    The integration requires these permissions:
    * Send emails on your behalf
    * View your email address
    * View your basic profile info
    
    We use minimal scopes to ensure security and privacy.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Using Gmail Integration

### Sending Quotes

Once connected, you can:

1. Select your Gmail account when sending quotes
2. Assign a default email for each of your locations in [Location Settings](/settings/general#default-email)

### Managing Connections

You can always remove an account by clicking the "Disconnect" button.

## Best Practices

1. Use a business Gmail account
2. Set your most-used account as default
3. Keep your email signature professional
