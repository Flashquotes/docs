Connect Gmail to send quotes from your business email and track customer conversations.

## What you can do

The Google integration allows you to send quotes and communications directly from your Gmail account. This creates a more personal connection with your leads and helps maintain your email communication history in one place.

<Tip>
Learn why [Google Workspace/Gmail is strongly recommended](/settings/integrations/email-provider) for the best Flashquotes experience.
</Tip>

## Connect Gmail

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to integrations">
    Navigate to Settings > Integrations
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Google account">
    Find the Google section and click "+ Add account"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    Sign in to your Google account and grant these permissions:
    - Send emails on your behalf
    - View your email address
    - View your basic profile info
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
You can connect multiple Gmail accounts if you need to send from different addresses.
</Note>

## Using the integration

**Sending quotes**:
- Select your Gmail account when sending quotes
- Set a default email for each location in [Location Settings](/settings/general#default-email)

**Managing connections**:
- View all connected accounts in Settings > Integrations
- Remove accounts by clicking "Disconnect"

## Best practices

1. Use a business Gmail account
2. Set your most-used account as default
3. Keep your email signature professional
