## Overview

The My Profile section allows you to manage your personal settings and control how you receive alerts and notifications from Flashquotes.

## Notification Management

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="bell" title="Alert Preferences">
    Control notifications for:
    - New bookings
    - Payment receipts
    - Lead submissions
    
    <Info>
      Each user can manage their own alert preferences independently of other team members.
    </Info>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="envelope" title="Email Settings">
    Configure email preferences:
    - Notification frequency
    - Alert types
    - Digest options
    - Priority levels
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Account Settings

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="user" title="Personal Information">

  <Accordion icon="lock" title="Security">

  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Best Practices

1. Keep contact information current
2. Review notification settings regularly
3. Enable important alerts
4. Configure time zone correctly
5. Maintain strong security measures
