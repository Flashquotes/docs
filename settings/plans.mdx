## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="credit-card" title="Can I change plans at any time?">
    Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from [Settings → Plans](https://app.flashquotes.com/settings/plans). When upgrading, you'll have immediate access to new features. When downgrading, you'll retain access until the end of your current billing period.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="clock" title="How does the free trial work?">
    Both Plus and Pro plans include a 14-day free trial. You'll have full access to all features during the trial. If you don't subscribe before the trial ends, you'll be moved to the Starter plan.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="users" title="Are there per-user fees?">
    No! All Flashquotes plans include unlimited team members at no additional cost.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="percent" title="Do you offer discounts?">
    Yes—annual billing saves 20% compared to monthly.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
