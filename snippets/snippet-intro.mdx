Flashquotes provides embeddable form snippets that allow you to integrate lead intake forms directly into your website. These snippets are the primary way to connect your website visitors with your Flashquotes booking system.

## Available Embed Options

Flashquotes currently supports form embedding through:

- **Iframe embeds** - Drop-in form widgets for any website
- **JavaScript embeds** - More flexible integration options
- **Share links** - Hosted form pages you can link to directly

<Note>
Only form embed snippets are available. General code snippets for other platform features are not currently supported.
</Note>

2. Select the form you want to embed
3. Click the **Publish** tab

## Common Use Cases

- Adding quote request forms to your website
- Creating landing pages for marketing campaigns
- Integrating with popular website builders (WordPress, Squarespace, Webflow)
- Generating QR codes for offline marketing materials
