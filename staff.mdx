## Overview

Flashquotes provides tools for managing your staff members, enabling team coordination and event assignments. While staff-facing features are currently focused on basic management, more features are planned for future updates.

## Staff Management

### Adding Staff Members

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="user-plus" title="Creating Staff Profiles">
    To add a new staff member:
    1. Navigate to the Staff tab
    2. Click `Add Staff` in the top right
    3. Enter required information:
       - First name
       - Last name
       - Email
       - Phone number
       - Role (e.g., Barista, Admin)
       - Status
       - Location
    
    <Note>
      For multi-city operations, staff location determines their availability for specific city events.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="phone" title="Staff Communication">
    Current staff features include:
    - Direct calling between team members via event brief
    - Basic contact management
    - Role assignments
    - Location-based availability
    
    <Info>
      Additional staff-facing tools are planned for future updates.
    </Info>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Best Practices

1. Keep staff contact information current
2. Assign appropriate roles and locations
3. Update availability status regularly
4. Ensure proper city assignments for multi-location operations
